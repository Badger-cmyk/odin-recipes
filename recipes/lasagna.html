Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich, savory ingredients like ground meat, cheese and tomato sauce.

Typically baked until bubbly and golden, it's known for its comforting, hearty flavors and creamy texture.

Ingredients

  • Lasana noodles
  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Cheese(preferrably mozzarella and Parmesan)
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven

    • Preheat your oven to 375 degree Fahrenheit

  2. Cook Noodles

    • Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  3. Prepare Meat Sauce

    • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.

    • Add chopped onion and cook until softened

    • Add minced garlic and cook for another minute

    • Add meat of your choice(beef or sausage). Cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.

    • Stir in tomato sauce. Season with salt, pepper and other seasonings of your choice and simmer for 10-15 minutes.

  4. Prepare Cheese Mixture

    • In a bowl, combine cheese with egg. Mix well and set aside.

  5. Assemble Lasagna

    • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.

    • Place a layer of noodles on top of the sauce.

    • Spread a portion of the cheese and egg mixture over the noodles.

    • Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

    • Repeat layers (meat sauce, noodles, cheese and egg mixture, mozzarella, Parmesan)

  6. Bake

    • Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the top is bubbly and golden.

  7. Serve

    • Let the lasagna cool for about 15 minutes before cutting into squares and serving.

    Enjoy your lasagna!
