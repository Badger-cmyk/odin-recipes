Slow-Cooker Pepper Steak

Description

Slow cooker pepper steak is a hearty and flavorful dish made with beef steak, bell peppers, onions, and a savory sauce, all cooked slowly in a slow cooker or Crock-Pot.

The slow cooking process tenderizes the beef and allows the flavors to meld together, resulting in a rich and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Beef Steak (1 to 1.5 pounds)
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients

    • Slice the beef steak into thin strips or bite-sized chunks.

    • Slice the bell peppers and onion into strips.

    • Mince the garlic.

  2. Brown the Beef (Optional)

    • Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.

    • Add the beef strips and cook until browned on all sides. This step adds extra flavor but can be skipped if you prefer a simpler method.

  3. Assemble in Slow Cooker

    • Place the sliced bell peppers, onions, and minced garlic in the bottom of the slow cooker.

    • If you browned the beef, add it to the slow cooker. If not, just add the raw beef directly.

    • Pour in the beef broth and soy sauce

    • Season with black pepper and ground ginger

  4. Cook

    • Cover the slow cooker and set it to cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours. The beef should be tender and the vegetables cooked through.

  5. Serve

    • Once cooking is complete, taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning if needed.

    • Serve the pepper steak over cooked rice or noodles.

    • Garnish with chopped green onions or sesame seeds if desired.

    Enjoy your meal!
